About Us

A & T Financial Advisers was established in early 2014 as an offshoot of the accounting practice of A & T Accountants and Advisers. At that time with legislative changes and other factors it was becoming increasingly obvious that clients wanted trusted but specialist financial advice particularly concerning retirement planning. We believed we had a solid foundation to service this market and our approach would be without the conflicted positions which were all too common in the industry in those days. Since then the firm has expanded and consolidated with other financial planning practices to form a practice which offers a full range of financial services. Whilst the firm has grown and changed we still believe that there is no "one size fits all" solution.

Each person's situation is unique, and therefore our approach comprises customisation and tailoring of our financial advice solutions to match the individual client's objectives. This is reflected in our motto “Planning for Your Success”. At A&T Financial Advisers our mission is to provide strategies to enhance your financial position and assist on your journey to wealth creation. We are a non-aligned advisory firm and not restricted by any affiliations to financial institutions or product providers. We pride ourselves on developing long-term cohesive relationships with our clients helping them achieve their financial goals and security.

Services

As qualified financial advisers, we are specialists in Insurance, Investment and Superannuation advice. We don’t accept a “one size fits all” service offering. We tailor our approach to you and your goals.

  • 01 Risk Protection

    Have you ever thought about what would happen to you and/or your family if the source of your income ceased?


    Insurance Cover
    • Life Insurance
    • TPD (Total Permanent Disability) Insurance
    • Trauma/Critical Illness insurance
    • Income Protection insurance

    Through our advice processes we can determine what your level of financial risk is and if cover is required what is the most suitable policy for your circumstances.


    You’re unlikely to need insurance cover forever and insurers often make changes to their policy terms just like any other service provider. As qualified advisers we are up to date on the insurance products on offer by most insurers which allows us to at least annually compare your existing policy(s) to ensure you are competitively covered.


    Insurance Claims

    The test of any insurance policy is best measured when a claim is made. We have considerable experience in this aspect and are able to expertly advise in the claim process from start to finish including:

    • Claim eligibility
    • Assistance with claim forms
    • Claim proceeds investment advice
  • 02 Investments & Wealth Solution

    Have you ever thought about how much you need to retire on or am I making the most out of my savings?

    These are common questions we are asked and the answer depends almost entirely on your goals, objectives and circumstances. Our advice processes will breakdown these questions into the various aspects they touch on and help you consider your options and build a picture as to what the future might look like. As we say “you can’t change your destination overnight but overnight you can change your direction ”

    We can offer practical advice on a range of financial matters including:


    Superannuation
    • Industry, Retail and SMSF Funds
    • Consolidation of superannuation accounts
    • Contribution limits
    • Health checks on performance & fees
    • First Home Super Saver Scheme (1st Home Buyers)

    Retirement Planning
    • Pre-retirement planning
    • Superannuation Pensions & Transition to Retirement (TTR)
    • Government Support entitlements

    Wealth Creation
    • Budgeting & managing cashflows
    • Developing Investment goals & plans
    • Wealth preservation & investment advice
    • Debt reduction & Tax structuring
    • Asset allocation & investment mix
    • Savings/education plans & Family assistance
  • 03 Estate Planning

    Have you ever thought about what happens if you no longer have mental capacity? Who can make decisions for you & what decisions can they make? Who controls your assets when you die?

    Not pleasant questions or matters to deal with but the consequences of not addressing them while you can are probably much more unpleasant.

    Everybody should have a Will and everybody should consider the need for an Enduring Power of Attorney and an Enduring Power of Guardianship.

    We offer services that can address these issues and provide insight as to the reasons why you need to consider these appointments as part of a wholistic financial plan.

    To the extent that legal advice is required we have trusted referral partners who can provide specific and tailored advice and prepare any necessary documentation.
